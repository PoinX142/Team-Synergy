What this chatbot does

This Interview Simulation Bot helps Polytechnic students practise Industry Immersion Programme (IIP) internship interviews.

  • Realistic interview questions
  • Feedback after each answer
  • Model answers for improvement
  • Final interview summary

How to start

  1. Provide your course, internship role, difficulty (easy / medium / hard), and number of questions
  2. Answer questions one by one
